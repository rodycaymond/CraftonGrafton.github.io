Welcome to Crafton Grafton

The image above is depicting the Dragonfly enemy encountered in-game.

The Crafton Grafton Attack Simulator is a text-based game where the player must defeat enemies of varying degrees in order to gain experience and level up, ultimately becoming strong enough to defeat Crafton Grafton! The player can visit the in-game item store to stock up on useful potions to help along the journey. Don't forget to rest between battles, or else risk dying mid fight!

How The Game Works

Create your character and fight monsters to level up and get stronger and eventually learn new attacks! You earn money after each victory which can be used in the Item Store to purchase helpful potions along your journey. Don't forget to rest every now and again so you're at full strength for your next battle!

Once you think you're strong enough, click the Final Boss Fight button to try and take down Crafton Grafton! But be warned! Crafton Grafton is no easy foe. Make sure you're stocked up on potions before entering battle!
